\begin{frame}{Project Overview: What I Am Building}
\begin{block}{Goal}
Build and evaluate fairness-aware routing policies for scarce-resource decisions under uncertainty and partial feedback.
\end{block}

\begin{itemize}
  \item Clinical side: route tests, retests, diagnostic attention, and triage escalation.
  \item Quantum side: route path capacity, qubit allocation, and entanglement-generation effort.
  \item Method comparison: MAB, CMAB, and iCMAB policies.
  \item Main fairness risk: missing or unreliable context creates repeated allocation harm.
\end{itemize}
\end{frame}
